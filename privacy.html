Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 September 2025 • Contact: tokidfrance@outlook.com

Summary

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect when you use the TikTok Automation app and how we use it. We only collect what is necessary to provide the service and to interact with TikTok APIs on your behalf.

Data we collect

  • Authentication data: information returned by TikTok OAuth (user id, username, access token) — used to identify and act on behalf of the user.
  • Profile data: basic profile fields required by the integration (display name, avatar URL) — used in-app for display and mapping.
  • Optional data: if the user explicitly grants extra scopes (for example posting media), data needed to perform that action.

How we use data

  • Authenticate you via TikTok OAuth and maintain a minimal user record to map workflows to accounts.
  • Perform user-authorized actions via TikTok API (only after explicit consent, per TikTok scopes requested).
  • Send transactional emails (account notifications) to tokidfrance@outlook.com as required.

Third parties & storage

We may use third-party hosting and analytics providers. We do not sell or lease your personal information. Access tokens are stored securely and rotated according to best practices.

  • Hosting: Netlify / GitHub Pages (static) or your hosting of choice.
  • Credentials: Access tokens stored only to perform user-authorized operations; we do not share tokens with other parties.

User rights

You can request the removal of your data or revoke tokens by contacting tokidfrance@outlook.com. We will remove personal data within a reasonable period after verification.

Use of TikTok API & requested scopes

When you connect your TikTok account, we request only the scopes necessary. Example mapping (fill exact scopes in TikTok console):

  • Basic profile scope: to read your id and display name so we can map actions to you.
  • Video/content publish scope (if requested): to upload or schedule videos only after your explicit approval in the UI.
  • Other read scopes: only to provide the functionality described in the app interface.

Security

We follow industry standard security practices. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact tokidfrance@outlook.com immediately.

Children

Our service is not directed to children under 13. If we discover data from a child under the applicable age, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will reflect changes.
