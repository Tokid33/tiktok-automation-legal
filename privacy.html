Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

We respect your privacy. Our app uses TikTok Login to authenticate users and only accesses data explicitly authorized by the user. We use the minimum scopes necessary: user.info.basic for profile display, and video-related scopes for uploading and publishing. Data is stored securely and used solely to provide the service. Users may request export or deletion of their data by contacting: tokidfrance@outlook.com.

Data retention & export

Creators can request a machine-readable export of profile and video statistics; export workflows follow GDPR-like principles and are delivered on request.
