Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how we process information when you use our demo website (“Service”) designed to showcase TikTok APIs (Login Kit, Content Posting API, Share Kit, Data Portability). This demo does not perform real publishing unless explicitly enabled and authorized.

1. Information We Process

  • OAuth basic profile (demo): If you authorize via TikTok, we may receive a temporary authorization code. In this demo environment, we do not exchange it for tokens or store it.

2. How We Use Information

  • To demonstrate login, permissions, and user flows required by TikTok App Review.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use personal data for targeted advertising.

4. Retention

This demo is designed not to persist user tokens or uploaded media. Any transient data is kept only as necessary for the session.
