Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

TikTok Automation ("we", "our", "the Service") provides a developer integration demo and tooling to create, manage and publish short-form videos via TikTok APIs. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • User profile data: display name, user id, and public profile fields when you authorize via OAuth.
  • Video metadata: titles, descriptions, and basic stats (views, likes) for exporting demo or analysis purposes.
  • Usage data: logs and analytics to improve the demo and troubleshoot issues (IP, timestamps, actions).

How we use data

We use collected data to demonstrate app functionality, export profile and video stats to authorized users, and to support debugging and product improvements. We do not share personal data with third parties except when required to operate the integration (TikTok API) or when legally required.

Data storage & retention

Demo data and exports are retained only as necessary to provide the service. You can request deletion by emailing tokidfrance@outlook.com. We will remove personal data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

Security

We use industry standard safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is perfectly secure—please do not submit highly sensitive data to the demo service.

Your choices

You can revoke access to the application via your TikTok account permissions. To request data export or deletion, contact tokidfrance@outlook.com.

Contact

Questions or requests: tokidfrance@outlook.com.
